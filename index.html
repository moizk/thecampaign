Chrissy Voskerichian files paperwork to run for City Council

Chrissy Voskerichian has filed paperwork with the New York City Campaign Finance Board to run for the New York City Council in the 19th District.

Chrissy is a lifelong resident of the Auburndale section of North Flushing. She has very strong ties to the community where she owns her home and raised her two children. Chrissy believes that her combination of her business, civic, and community experience makes her an ideal candidate for City Council.

Welcome to my website!

I am running for City Council to make a difference for Northeast Queens. My entire life I have worked hard and done my best to help people, and help our community, and now I am prepared to take it to the next level.

This campaign is not about me, it is about protecting our quality of life and serving the residents of Northeast Queens, a community where I grew up and where I have lived my entire life. I raised my family in this neighborhood, I bought my house in this neighborhood, I love this neighborhood.
