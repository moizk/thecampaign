News

Chrissy Calls on Council to Uphold Mayor's Veto of "Community Safety Act"

Chrissy Voskerichian, candidate for City Council in Northeast Queens, called upon members of the City Council to vote against Intro 1079 and 1080, collectively known as the "Community Safety Act."

Chrissy Calls on MTA to Revise Financial Projections from Latest Budget

Chrissy Voskerichian, candidate for City Council in Northeast Queens, called on the Metropolitan Transportation Authority (MTA) to revise its budget and financial projections in order to reduce or eliminate the need to increase transit fares.

Chrissy Voskerichian Signs Participatory Budgeting Pledge, Urges Other Candidates To Do The Same

Chrissy Voskerichian, candidate for City Council in Northeast Queens, signed a pledge that if elected she will continue to use Participatory Budgeting as a method of allocating $1 Million of the capital budget.

Chrissy Qualifies for Maximum Matching Funds

Chrissy Voskerichian, candidate for City Council in Northeast Queens, has raised enough money to qualify for the maximum amount of matching funds permitted by the Campaign Finance Board. Chrissy has raised $15,920 from New York City residents as of July 11th, which exceeds the threshold required to receive $92,400 from the City's matching funds program.

Chrissy First in City to File Petitions

Chrissy Voskerichian, candidate for City Council in Northeast Queens, was the first candidate for any office in New York City to file designating petitions in order to secure a place on the ballot for the September primary. Today, July 8, was the first day to file petitions with the Board of Elections' Executive Office in lower Manhattan.

Chrissy Fears Council Bill Would Make Policing Impossible

Chrissy Voskerichian, candidate for City Council in Northeast Queens, announced her opposition to a bill currently being considered in the City Council that would make it illegal for a police officer to identify a suspect's age, gender, color or disability. Officers would be limited to providing descriptions of suspects based only on their clothing.

Chrissy raises $11000, Qualifies for Matching Funds in 11 Days

Chrissy Voskerichian, candidate for City Council in Northeast Queens filed her first disclosure with the Campaign Finance Board (CFB) this morning. Since registering with CFB on April 30th Chrissy has raised over $11,000 including meeting the required thresholds to qualify for the City's matching funds program.

Chrissy supports Flushing Airport Park, Not as part of Soccer Deal

Chrissy Voskerichian, candidate for City Council in Northeast Queens, supports the idea of turning Flushing Airport in College Point into a park, but not as part of a land trade that would build a soccer stadium in Flushing Meadows Park.

Chrissy Voskerichian files paperwork to run for City Council

Chrissy Voskerichian has filed paperwork with the New York City Campaign Finance Board to run for the New York City Council in the 19th District.
