Chrissy's Statement on NYPD Gun Bust

Chrissy Voskerichian, candidate for City Council in Northeast Queens, released the following statement about the NYPD's arrest of 19 individuals for smuggling more than 250 illegal guns into New York City. Chrissy has served as President of the 109th Precinct Community Council since 2009 and is an outspoken advocate of proactive policing as a tool to prevent crime.

"Thanks to the excellent work of Commissioner Ray Kelly along with the men and women of the NYPD our City is now a little safer" Chrissy said. "These guns were going to be used to rob and kill people and now they are off the street, and the criminals are behind bars. Most telling is the fact that one suspect was afraid to bring the illegal guns into New York City because he knew he would be caught if he were to be stopped, questioned and frisked. Proactive policing saves lives, we must give the NYPD every resource available to keep us safe."
