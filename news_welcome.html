Welcome to my website!

I am running for City Council to make a difference for Northeast Queens. My entire life I have worked hard and done my best to help people, and help our community, and now I am prepared to take it to the next level.

This campaign is not about me, it is about protecting our quality of life and serving the residents of Northeast Queens, a community where I grew up and where I have lived my entire life. I raised my family in this neighborhood, I bought my house in this neighborhood, I love this neighborhood.

I am running for City Council because I want to make our neighborhood better. I am not a politician, I have never run for office and I never planed to run for office. I am a businesswoman and a civic leader. I am used to fighting for what I believe in, and I am prepared for fight for Northeast Queens.

I hope that you will join me and vote for me. If everyone works together there is no telling how much good we can do for our community.

- Chrissy
